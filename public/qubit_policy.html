Privacy Policy for Qubit – WhatsApp Business Integration

Effective Date: August 2025

1. Introduction

Qubit (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you log in to our application integrated with WhatsApp via the Meta Business API.

By using our login system and WhatsApp integration, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you log in or interact with our application via WhatsApp, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Identity Data: Your name, phone number, and any identifiers used to authenticate you.
  • Contact Data: Phone number associated with WhatsApp account.
  • Usage Data: Messages exchanged via WhatsApp, timestamps, interaction logs.
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP address, browser type.

We do not collect or store your WhatsApp contacts or messages beyond what is necessary to provide our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate and authorize your access to the Qubit platform.
  • Facilitate communication between you and Qubit via WhatsApp.
  • Provide personalized support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Improve and monitor our service quality and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or to trusted service providers bound by confidentiality.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements. When no longer needed, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Data portability.

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the support channels available in the Qubit application.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Use of Cookies and Tracking

Our login dialog may use cookies or similar technologies strictly for authentication and session management purposes.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes via the app or other communication channels.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please use the support features within the Qubit application.
